Risk Assessment

We conduct comprehensive risk assessments to identify potential hazards in the workplace. Our team evaluates risks associated with machinery, chemicals, and other workplace activities, providing actionable recommendations to ensure a safe environment for all employees.

Workplace Safety

We develop and implement customized workplace safety programs to meet your organization's needs. From safety policy creation to emergency response planning, we ensure your workplace complies with local and international safety standards.

Safety Training

We offer tailored safety training programs for employees and management. Our training covers essential topics such as fire safety, first aid, hazardous material handling, and more. Our goal is to empower your team with the knowledge to maintain a safe and compliant workplace.

Compliance Audits

We conduct detailed compliance audits to ensure your organization meets all regulatory requirements. Our audits cover OSHA standards, NEBOSH guidelines, and other relevant safety regulations. We provide actionable insights to improve compliance and safety performance.

Safety Committees

We assist organizations in establishing and managing safety committees to foster a culture of safety. Our services include committee formation, training, and ongoing support to ensure effective communication and collaboration on safety matters.

OUR EXPERIENCE

Building a safer workplace since 2022

Occupational Health and Safety Training

2022 - Present

Since our establishment in 2022, Mission Safety Consult has been providing high-quality occupational health and safety training to businesses and organizations across Ghana. We have partnered with reputable institutions such as the Korea International Cooperation Agency (KOICA) and the Suame Vocational Training Institute to deliver impactful safety programs.

Safety Audits and Risk Assessments

2022 - Present

We have conducted over 120 safety audits for various organizations, identifying potential hazards and providing actionable recommendations to mitigate risks. Our expertise in risk management has helped businesses create safer work environments and reduce workplace accidents.

Safety Training Programs

2022 - Present

We have trained over 500 employees across multiple industries, including construction, manufacturing, and vocational training. Our training programs cover essential topics such as fire safety, first aid, and hazardous material handling, empowering employees with the knowledge to maintain a safe workplace.

Compliance and Regulatory Support

2022 - Present

We have assisted numerous organizations in achieving compliance with local and international safety regulations, including OSHA and NEBOSH standards. Our compliance audits have resulted in a 98% success rate in meeting regulatory requirements.

Safety Culture Development

2022 - Present

We have helped organizations establish and manage safety committees to promote a culture of safety. Our ongoing support ensures effective communication and collaboration on safety matters, leading to improved workplace safety performance.
